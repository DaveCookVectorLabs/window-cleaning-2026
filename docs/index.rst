Window Cleaning Cost Calculator
================================

A free, open-source window cleaning cost calculator for commercial facility managers, property managers, and building operations teams. Built and maintained by `Binx Professional Cleaning <https://www.binx.ca/>`_ — a WSIB-covered, fully insured commercial and residential cleaning company operating in Northern Ontario since 2013.

This tool helps facility managers estimate window cleaning costs for commercial buildings, office towers, retail storefronts, medical facilities, and multi-unit residential properties across North Bay, Sudbury, Timmins, Sault Ste. Marie, and surrounding communities.
